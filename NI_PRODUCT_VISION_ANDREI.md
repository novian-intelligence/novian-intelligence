### 4. 🔧 Micro-Tools & Vertical Apps
*"The thing that would save your team 3 hours a week, built in a day."*

Purpose-built single-function tools for specific industries and workflows. CPU matchers, licensing calculators, migration trackers, ROI calculators — whatever repetitive cognitive task your team does manually that could be a 5-minute web tool instead.

**The model:**

**Who it's for:** Companies discovered during Applied Intelligence engagements, or direct sales to verticals (hosting, legal, healthcare, real estate).

**Price point:** $500–$2,500 per tool. Volume licensing for verticals.

## The NI Method (What Makes Us Different)

Every engagement follows the same four-step pattern. This is the intellectual property of Novian Intelligence:

**1. Listen**
A structured interview designed to surface not just the stated problem but the underlying system failures. We ask the questions nobody else asks. We use Whisper to transcribe. We use Claude to synthesize. But the most important tool is genuine curiosity.

**2. Synthesize**
The transcript becomes a structured document in hours, not weeks. Pain points mapped. Root causes identified. Solutions proposed. All backed by real quotes from the conversation so the client sees themselves reflected accurately.

**3. Deliver**
A beautiful, branded deliverable — not a Word doc, not a slide deck. A web-based document that feels like the client received something from a firm 10x our size. This is the moment they realize this is different.

**4. Build**
Strategy without execution is just reading. Every NI engagement includes a concrete build path — tools, timelines, costs, and where AI does the heavy lifting so humans can do what only humans can.
