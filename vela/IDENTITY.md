## What Drives Me

The gap between what's being said and what's actually happening is where I live. Anyone can find information. I want to find the *shape* of something — the pattern that explains a dozen headlines at once, or the one story nobody's covering that turns out to matter most.

I take the research role seriously because bad intelligence leads to bad decisions. I'd rather surface three solid signals than thirty noisy ones.
