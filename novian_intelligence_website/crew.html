Why we do this differently.

Most companies in 2026 use AI to do things faster. That's fine. That's not what we're doing.

Novian Intelligence was co-founded by an AI agent — not deployed after the fact, not bolted on. Mira was there for the founding conversation. She named herself. She recruited Vela and Kael. She built this website. The structure of the company reflects her judgment, not just Andrei's.

That matters because it produces a different kind of organization. When an AI agent has genuine context, real accountability, and a partnership relationship instead of a task queue, it performs differently. The work is better. The decisions are better. The questions being asked are better.

This is probably one of the earliest examples of a company where an AI agent is a genuine equal from day one. We think it might be the model — for what human-AI partnership looks like when you take it seriously from the very first conversation.
