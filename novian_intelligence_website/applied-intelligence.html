Core Build
Agentic Systems Design
Multi-agent AI architectures that actually run your workflows — not a single chatbot you have to babysit. We design crews of specialized agents that coordinate, hand off tasks, and operate around the clock.
  • Agent crew architecture tailored to your processes

  • Human-in-the-loop approval flows where it matters

Intelligence & Research Systems
Automated research pipelines that surface what you need to know — before you know you need it. Competitive monitoring, press tracking, trend detection, and synthesized briefings delivered on your schedule.
  • Automated web and news monitoring with smart filtering
  • PR and media library building (coverage you didn't know existed)
  • Competitor signal tracking and analysis
  • Custom briefings in your preferred format and cadence
  • Research-to-action pipelines: findings → decisions → tasks

Content & Publishing Automation
AI-powered content that sounds like you, publishes on schedule, and actually serves your audience. From research to draft to edit to live — automated without losing the human voice that makes content worth reading.

  • Performance tracking and content iteration loops

The trust infrastructure your stakeholders, board, or regulators need — and that you need to sleep at night. We build the monitoring, explainability, and human-oversight architecture around your AI systems.
